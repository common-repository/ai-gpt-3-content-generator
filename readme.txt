GPT based AI content writer for WordPress, create content, generate images, automatic content creator ChatGPT content writer.

== Description ==
Automate your content generation process for WordPress using the power of AI. With GPT-based AI models, generate engaging and high-quality content effortlessly. You can also create featured images for your posts, pages, and custom posts, and ensure that your content is error-free with built-in proofreading tools. Additionally, you can translate your content into multiple languages, enabling you to reach a wider audience and expand your reach.

== Features ==
* Content Writer: Create high-quality, engaging content with ease by utilizing the power of AI-based content generation. Our GPT-based content generator will help you generate fresh, unique content that captivates your audience and keeps them coming back for more.
* Image Generator (DALL-E): Bring your content to life with stunning, custom-generated images for your posts. Our plugin uses DALL-E to create unique and visually appealing images, which you can easily save to your WordPress site and add to your content.
* Title Suggestion: Struggling to come up with catchy titles for your blog posts? Let our plugin suggest the perfect title for your content. Our GPT-based AI will analyze your content and generate a range of title suggestions to choose from.
* Excerpt generation: Summarize your content with the help of our AI-powered excerpt generator. Our plugin will analyze your content and create a concise and informative summary, which you can use as your post excerpt.
* Content Proofreading: Ensure your content is error-free and of the highest quality with our built-in proofreading tools. Our plugin uses AI to identify and correct any mistakes, helping you create polished and professional content.
* Translations: Reach a global audience with ease by using our translation feature. Our plugin can translate your content into over 38 different languages, helping you expand your reach and connect with new audiences.
* SEO Suggestions and Optimizations: Get expert advice on how to optimize your content for search engines with our SEO suggestions feature. Our plugin will analyze your content and provide suggestions for optimizing your titles, keywords, and more.
* AI Playground:  Explore the full potential of AI with our plugin's AI Playground feature. Experiment with tasks such as translation, proofreading, summarization, and SEO to see what works best for your content.
* WordPress Assistant: Get all your WordPress-related queries answered quickly and efficiently with our AI-powered WordPress Assistant. Our plugin's assistant is always on hand to help with any WordPress-related issues you may encounter.

== Installation ==
= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Visit `Plugins > Add New`.
2. Search for `AI GPT-3 Content Generator`. Find and Install `AI GPT-3 Content Generator`.
3. Activate the plugin from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =
1. Download the AI GPT-3 Content Generator plugin.
2. Unzip and upload the `ai-gpt3-content-generator` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate the AI GPT-3 Content Generator from your Plugins page.

== Open AI ==
This plugin utilizes the API from [OpenAI](https://beta.openai.com). This plugin does not gather any information from your OpenAI account except for the number of tokens utilized. The data transmitted to the OpenAI servers primarily consists of the content of your article and the context you specify. Any and all communications during content generation are strictly between OpenAI and your WordPress site. Please also review their [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/) and [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/terms/) for further information.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
=Where do I get the API key?=
1. Create an account at OpenAI.
2. Create an API key [view your API Keys](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys) and insert it in the plugin settings (Plugin Settings tab > API Key).
3. Always keep an eye on [your OpenAI usage](https://beta.openai.com/account/usage)!

=Content generation taking too long.=
Good things take time. Content generation usually takes time because the total time required for the model to compute and generate your content is high.

=How do I generate content for a WordPress site using OpenAI ChatGPT/GPT 3?=
To generate content for a WordPress site using OpenAI's ChatGPT or GPT-3 API, you would need to follow these general steps:

1. Get an OpenAI API key: You would first need to obtain an API key from OpenAI to access their API services. You can apply for an API key on OpenAI's website.

2. Install the <b>AI GPT-3 Content Generator</b> plugin and enter your API key in the plugin, choose your preferred GPT-3 AI model.

3. Navigate to your post editor and you will find the title/content/excerpt generation options on the right side metabox. Choose the preferred option and generate your content.
